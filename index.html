Golf GTI

Volkswagen GTI 0-60 MPH

Under the hood, you will find a 2.0L turbo direct-injection engine that has been designed to be both powerful and efficient. To boost performance, this engine forces cool air directly to the engine. When you fill-up the tank with premium gasoline, you can expect 228 hp and 258 lb-ft of torque. According to tests performed by Car and Driver, the Volkswagen GTI 0-60 mph times are quite remarkable. This vehicle can hit that mark in just 5.7 seconds! It can travel a quarter-mile in only 14.3 seconds, and the Golf GTI can reach speeds of 100 mph.

Additional Performance Highlights of the Volkswagen Golf GTI
  • DCC® Adaptive Chassis Control: Choose from three different levels of suspension firmness with this optional amenity. DCC® controls the damper valves by receiving signals from wheel and acceleration sensors.
  • Progressive Power Steering:This system quickly responds to input from the driver, even at high speeds. It also makes commuting more convenient when you are driving at lower speeds.
  • Paddle Shifters: Opt for the available paddle shifters, and you won’t have to take your hands off of the wheel to control the 7-speed DSG® transmission.
  • GTI Performance Brakes:For optimal stopping power, choose the ventilated disc brakes with the GTI logo.
